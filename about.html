Imagine being able to feel the sunshine on your face when you wake up, being able to move freely when you make breakfast,
and it just being incredibly easy to get dressed and out the door in a jiffy!

We - Pagi Studio - may be interior designers but more importantly we love the experience of life at the crack of dawn.
As the word "Pagi" means morning in Bahasa Indonesia, our philosophy in design derives from the gentleness and warmtn you felt in the morning.

Spaces to us are really important to making sure we feel enlivened, re-energised and raring to start a new day's adventure.
Be it space for you to open your eyes firstly in the morning, or to please the eyes of customers who first stepped into your place.
We want to make sure the spaces you live and adventure within are curated in a way that makes you feel connected.
Each space we design is measured to glow with specific needs in mind

So come connect with us, and share your story!
